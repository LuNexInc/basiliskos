# Third-party notices

Basiliskos downloads and bundles the Windows AMD64 release of
[CLIProxyAPI](https://github.com/router-for-me/CLIProxyAPI) v7.2.77 as its local
protocol gateway. The archive and executable are pinned by SHA-256 and the
runtime verifies the executable again before launching it.

CLIProxyAPI is licensed under the MIT License. Its required notice is included
at `src-tauri/resources/gateway/LICENSE.CLIProxyAPI.txt` and in packaged app
resources.
